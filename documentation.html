Downloadable CSV

The datasets used in this project were carefully selected and processed to ensure they align with the objectives of our study. Each dataset was cleaned, formatted, and supplemented to provide the most accurate and relevant information about Australian water consumption. The datasets include data on water usage by state, territory, and sector, spanning from 2015 to 2022. The processed datasets are provided here for easy access and download.
